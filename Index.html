What is AutoMeet?

Tired of constantly messaging your friends for meetings or hangouts? We are here to help!

AutoMeet allows you to use your Google Calendar and Facebook contact list to find mutual times for you and your friends to meet, for whatever reason.

One Step Sign-in

Tired of all the complicated registrations and the spam emails? No worries. We've got you covered.

Sign in with only one click. With your Facebook contact list and your Google calendar, everything is clear, straightforward and eays.
